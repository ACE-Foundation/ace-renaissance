# **USE CASE: ACE vs. Traditional Crypto Mining**  
### *A top‑down comparison of two fundamentally different value‑creation models*

## **1. Executive Summary**
Traditional crypto mining converts **electricity** into **artificial digital assets**.  
ACE‑Mining converts **real‑world climate action** into **fungible CO₂e ownership tokens**.

Both systems “mint” tokens.  
Only one of them produces **real value**.

---

## **2. System Inputs**

### **Crypto Mining**
- Electricity  
- Hardware  
- Hash computations  

**Nature of input:**  
Pure energy consumption with no external utility.

### **ACE**
- Agronomic improvements  
- Land stewardship  
- Soil carbon enhancement  
- Biomass management  
- Biochar production  
- BECCS / DACCS (energy‑based removals)  
- Capital investments in climate‑positive systems  

**Nature of input:**  
Real, measurable climate‑positive work grounded in biology, physics, and engineering.

---

## **3. Core Mechanism**

### **Crypto Mining**
- Solve arbitrary cryptographic puzzles  
- Compete for block rewards  
- Produce tokens backed only by scarcity and belief  

**Mechanism:**  
Closed‑loop computation with no external value creation.

### **ACE**
- Run a deterministic, certifiable carbon engine  
- Quantify biogenic, fossil, and negative CO₂e flows  
- Produce a verifiable climate certificate  
- Anchor it to a ledger  
- Mint fungible CO₂e tokens  

**Mechanism:**  
Open‑loop transformation of real climate impact into a standardized asset.

---

## **4. Output**

### **Crypto Mining**
- Token with no intrinsic value  
- No physical counterpart  
- No measurable external benefit  

### **ACE**
- Verified CO₂e reduction  
- Ledger‑anchored certificate  
- Fungible CO₂e tokens (1 token = 1 kg CO₂e)  
- Full traceability:  
  **Token → Ledger → Certificate → Engine → Real‑world climate work**

---

## **5. Economic Model**

### **Crypto**
- Token value = market psychology  
- Mining reward = energy burn  
- No connection to real assets  

### **ACE**
- Token value = verified CO₂e reduction  
- Mining reward = climate‑positive work  
- Backed by measurable environmental benefit  

---

## **6. Environmental Impact**

### **Crypto**

- No climate benefit  
- Often negative externalities  

### **ACE**
- Soil carbon increase  
- Biomass accumulation  
- Fossil displacement  
- Engineered removals (DACCS/BECCS)  
- Net climate benefit  

---

## **7. Liquidity & Fungibility**

### **Crypto**
- Fungible tokens  
- Liquidity depends on speculation  

### **ACE**
- Single‑class fungible CO₂e token  
- 1 token = 1 kg CO₂e  
- Decimal remainders stay in certificates and accumulate  
- Liquidity based on standardized climate value  

---

## **8. Why ACE‑Mining Is “Mining for the Real World”**
ACE‑Mining is the first system where:

- **Input = real climate work**  
- **Output = a fungible, ledger‑anchored CO₂e asset**  
- **Value = grounded in physical reality**  

Crypto mining produces artificial scarcity.  
ACE produces **verifiable climate benefit**
